Your Team of AI Assistants

What is an AI Assistant?

An AI assistant (or Large Language Model) is a powerful computer program designed to understand and generate human-like text. Think of it as a super-smart chatbot that has read a massive portion of the internet.

You can ask it questions in plain English, have it explain complex topics, help you brainstorm, write code, find bugs, and much more. It‚Äôs like having a team of experts on call 24/7 to help you learn and create.

Modern programming and data analysis are rarely done alone. In the past, you might have had to rely on a textbook or a single instructor. Today, you have access to a suite of powerful AI assistants that are like having a team of experts and a massive, interactive library available to you 24/7.

We are having you sign up for several because, just like people, each AI has different strengths. You will learn which one to ask for different types of questions.

Tip

Use Your University Email

When possible, sign up for these services using your university email address. Many services offer free educational plans or additional credits for students.

Protect Your Privacy

These AI models learn from the conversations you have. Never share private or sensitive information with them, such as passwords, personal identification, or confidential data. Treat every chat as if it were public.
